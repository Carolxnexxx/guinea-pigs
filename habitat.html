Guinea Pig Habitats

Guinea pigs are one of the largest rodent pets, so they need lots of room!

Guinea pigs cannot jump or climb, so ramps and platforms can provide variety.

Even though they live mostly in their cage, they should be brought outside to explore at least once a day!

A large cage means that guinea pigs don't get bored.

It also means that they are less likely to develop dangerous medical conditions.

A good guinea pig habitat means a happy and healthy guinea pig!
