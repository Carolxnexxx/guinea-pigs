Guinea Pig Diet

Guinea pigs need lots of different foods in their diets.

First, they need grass hay. It should make up around 80% of their diet!

Second, they need pellets since they have vitamin C!

Third, they love to eat fresh vegetables such as lettuce, kale, and green peppers.

Finally, guinea pigs can eat fruit for a sweet treat!
